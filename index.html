Supporting users, systems and the infrastructure behind them.

My role covers the day-to-day support users rely on, but it also includes maintaining systems, improving security, supporting infrastructure and finding better ways for IT to operate.

I am an IT Support Executive working across a broad range of business technology. On any given day, I may be troubleshooting a user issue, administering Microsoft 365 or Active Directory, investigating a network or VoIP problem, maintaining backup and storage systems, supporting business-critical software, or improving an internal process.

The part I enjoy most is understanding why a problem keeps happening and then creating a cleaner, more reliable way to solve it. That might involve changing a process, strengthening a configuration, improving documentation, building an automation or working with colleagues to prevent the issue from returning.

I am continuing to develop my knowledge through certifications, personal projects and hands-on learning. Azure is one area I am currently learning, alongside the wider infrastructure, security and automation skills I use throughout my role.

What I do

01

User & business support

02

03

Infrastructure & servers

Support Windows Server, Group Policy, DNS, DHCP, file services, virtualisation and business-critical infrastructure.

04

Networking & VoIP

Troubleshoot switching, VLANs, wireless networks, connectivity and VoIP or contact-centre platforms including 8x8, ConnexOne and NICE CXone.

05

Security & monitoring

Support endpoint security, vulnerability remediation, patching, infrastructure monitoring and Cyber Essentials improvement work.

06

Backup & storage

Monitor and maintain Veeam backups, perform restores and support QNAP storage, RAID, snapshots, iSCSI and permissions.

07

Software & applications

Deploy, configure and troubleshoot desktop software, specialist applications and department-specific platforms.

08

Automation & reporting

Build and support UiPath automations, PowerShell scripts and Power BI reporting that reduce manual work and improve visibility.

09

Process improvement

Create documentation, knowledge articles, training and better workflows to improve consistency and reduce repeat support demand.
